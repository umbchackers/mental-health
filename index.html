What is Imposter Syndrome?

Imposter Syndrome is the feeling that we haven't earned our accomplishments, or that our ideas or creations aren't worth other's time.

Watch the TED-Ed video about Imposter Syndrome.

How can I overcome my Imposter Syndrome?

  • Know that you're not alone.
  • Think about what type of Imposter Syndrome you have.
  • Try not to compare yourself to others, everyone is in different situations and comes from different backgrounds.
  • Think about how much you know. (Imposter Syndrome vs the Dunning-Kruger Effect).

How can I stay healthy at the hackathon?

  • Take screen breaks, stand up and walk around, get fresh air.
  • Eat healthy foods, drink water!
  • Talk to the people around you.
  • Don't let the stress of completion get to you, as long as you're learning, you've already accomplished something. You can always finish the program later.
  • Try Meditation or Chair Yoga.
